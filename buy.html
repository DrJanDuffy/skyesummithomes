New Construction vs. Resale in Skye Summit

New Construction

  • Latest floor plans and finishes
  • Builder warranties included
  • Choose your lot and upgrades
  • Energy-efficient features
  • Longer timeline (6-12 months)
  • Limited negotiation on base price

Resale

  • Move-in ready immediately
  • Established landscaping/mature trees
  • More negotiation flexibility
  • See exactly what you're buying
  • May need updates/renovations
  • Older systems may need attention

I'll help you decide which option fits your timeline, budget, and lifestyle goals in Skye Summit.
